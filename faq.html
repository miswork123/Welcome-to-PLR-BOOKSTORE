  • What is PLR?

    PLR (abbreviation for "Private Label Rights") is a product for which you pay a one-time fee for a license to use and make 100% profits from it. You can add your own name to the products (rebranding) and sell or give them away to your audience without needing the author's permission.

    Some PLR licenses may differ, so it's always best to double-check the license terms for each product.

  • Are there any restrictions I can and can't do with PLR products?

    Each product has different licensing. The best way to find out about the product’s license is by looking at the product’s license descriptions outlined in the product’s detail page. The use of the product licenses can be found in the explanations given below:

    1. What is Resale Rights (RR)? The resale license allows you to resell the product, but you cannot pass on this resell right to your customers. It's similar to having your own online store. 

    2. What is Master Resell Rights (MRR)? This license allows you to resell the product to your customers but it doesn’t stop there, it also gives your customers the right to resell the product to their customers.

    3. What is Private Label Rights (PLR)? Private Label Rights (PLR) provides you the authority to claim the ownership of the product and resell them to your customers. You have the complete freedom to do whatever you want with the product that you purchased. It's as if you simply declared yourself to be Facebook’s creator, and Meta wouldn't dare to argue with you. In other words, you've just made your own products without having to lift a finger.

    4. What is Give Away Right? This license grants you the right to give away the items for free to the public. You can share, distribute across the internet, give away to your subscribers, completely for free as you wish.
